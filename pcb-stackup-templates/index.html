Important Notices & Disclaimer

For reference and educational purposes only. These stackup templates are generalized recommendations based on established EMI/EMC design principles. They do not constitute professional engineering advice for any specific product or application.

Always validate with your PCB manufacturer. Actual dielectric thicknesses, material availability, minimum copper weights, and impedance targets vary by fabricator. Run field-solver impedance simulations (e.g., Polar SI, Simbeor, or your EDA tool's built-in solver) before committing to production.

Compliance responsibility. EMC certification (FCC, CE, CISPR, etc.) depends on the complete system design — not only the stackup. Proper component placement, routing, decoupling, grounding, shielding, and cable management are equally critical.
